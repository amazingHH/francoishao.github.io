---

## 🔬 Research Focus

I work on:

- Powerline communication (PLC) through battery cells
- Signal integrity under electrochemical variation
- State-of-Health (SOH) monitoring using communication signals
- Scalable battery network architectures

---

## 📌 Current Work

I am currently investigating how PLC performance varies across different battery chemistries and cycling conditions, and how this can be used for real-time diagnostics in large battery packs.
